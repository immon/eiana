IANA Root Zone Management

The IANA provides two different mechanisms for operators of top-level domains to alter their records in the IANA Root Zone Database. We recommend the use of our automated online system which provides for immediate feedback, online tracking of requests and the fastest turnaround times. Alternatively, the traditional template can be used and sent through email, post of fax.

Login to automated system

Registered users of the automated system may provide their username and password. If you are a listed contact for a domain in the IANA database, but do not have a username and password, please contact IANA Root Management for assistance.

Traditional Template

You may alternatively opt to submit a request by filling out our text template. You may submit this form via email to root-mgmt@iana.org (recommended), or via post or fax to IANA. Please note that processing can take longer with this option as more manual handling is required.
